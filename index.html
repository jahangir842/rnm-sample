⇄ Move Funds

Withdraw on-chain BTC between Core Nodes or to a manually listed external URI. Records a withdrawal fee on the next layer.
🔒 Requires OTP confirmation before broadcast

⊟ Splice Funds

Splice in/out of off-chain capacity on a Core Node channel. Records a splicing fee. Liquidity is in-limbo until confirmed.

⊗ Channel Management

Open or close channels on a Core Node. Closure records a closure fee on the next layer.
🔒 Requires OTP confirmation

↓ Deposit Reporting

Inform the system of an inbound deposit so the oracle ledger stays reconciled. Records a deposit fee on the next layer.
